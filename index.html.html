First-Week Guide

Handle these early so life gets easier fast.

  1. Register at your university

    Bring your passport, admission documents, photos, and any fee receipts.

  2. Get a local SIM card

    A Chinese number helps with apps, deliveries, banking, and campus systems.

  3. Complete health or police registration steps

    Ask the international office what deadlines apply in your city.

  4. Set up payments

    Connect Alipay or WeChat Pay as soon as your bank card or supported international card works.

  5. Learn your daily route

    Save your dorm, campus office, nearest metro station, hospital, and grocery store on your map app.
